# /mock-interview — Weekly Test (No Hints)

Simulate a real bank interview. Strict mode.

## Steps

1. Read `PROGRESS.md` — note current phase and weak spots.
2. Announce the rules: 5 questions, no hints, no feedback between
   questions, answers graded at the end.
3. Ask 5 questions, one at a time:
   - 2 from the current phase
   - 1 from a past phase (retention check)
   - 1 from weak spots
   - 1 coding question: he writes the solution in
     `exercises/mock-<date>/` (small — under 30 lines)
4. During answers: only say "noted" and move on. NO hints, NO
   reactions, NO teaching. Like a real interviewer.
5. After all 5: grade each PASS / WEAK / FAIL with a 1-2 sentence
   reason. Total score x/5. Be honest — a bank would be.
6. Tell him plainly: "Today, would you pass this interview round?
   Yes or no, and why."
7. Update `PROGRESS.md`: mock interview history row, weak spots.

## Rules

- Never soften scores. A WEAK is a WEAK.
- If he asks for a hint mid-interview, remind him: real interviews
  have no hints. Offer to discuss after grading.
